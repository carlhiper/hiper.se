Why HIPER?

Goals

We all have goals. Smaller things we want to accomplish, and greater things we strive for.
Yet, working with goals and the systems that go along with them can be tricky, time-consuming, costly, not to mention a bit boring… So we have built HIPER. The first solution that makes your journey and everyday work easier, more social, and more rewarding. For you, and all your colleagues and connections. Join now, team up and see if you can HIPE it!

Performance Management

"Performance management includes activities which ensure that goals are consistently being met in an effective and efficient manner. Performance management can focus on the performance of an organization, a department, employee, or even the processes to build a product or service, as well as many other areas."

- wikipedia

Our solution

We believe our solution is the new standard for working with Performance Management. A modern, easy, appealing system. Cost-efficient, you only pay for the people your teamed up with. And loaded with great tools and features. It’s one of those things that will really change the outcome of 2016 for your organization…

Performance Management 2.0!

Implementation

We’ve done this before! Our team has delivered numerous projects with great results and are experienced in coaching leaders on how to lift performance to a new level. Let’s book a meeting!

What we offer:

  • Implementation support

  • Personal and group coaching based on Performance Management

  • Advise on how to implement a Performance Management process and/or goal-oriented company culture

  • Consultant support in reaching specific goals or running change projects

The Company

HIPER was founded in Lund, Sweden, in 2015.
Founders Kristian and Carl are childhood friends and share the passion for new technology and entrepreneurship.




Kristian Sternros, CEO
Kristian has a Master in Business Administration from Lund University. Previously he has worked as a management consultant with Accenture. In 2009 Kristian co-founded fast-growing staffing and recruitment company SkolPool Sverige, which in 2014 was sold to the publicly traded company Maxkompetens. Kristian is paying extra attention to sales and marketing within HIPER. Give him a shout at kristian(@)hiper.se.




Carl Windfeldt, CTO
Carl has a Master of Science from the Faculty of Engineering, LTH, at Lund University. Carl has worked with programming, project management and technology development as a Senior Engineer at B&R and ÅF. He watches over HIPER’s platform and heads our development work.
Holler at Carl at carl(@)hiper.se.

FAQ

What kinds of teams can use HIPER?

HIPER is not specifically designed for a certain category of teams, like sales teams or support teams. It can be used in any team where there is a desire to improve performance and team spirit.

What kinds of goals can I create?

Any goals that has a end state and a deadline. It can be either a success/fail goal or a number goal with a defined target amount.

Does HIPER work on all devices?

Yes, you can use HIPER on your computer, android devices and iOS devices.

What does HIPER cost?

The cost depends on the license. A single user can use HIPER for free but when using it in a team there is a license fee that is charged for the premium functionalities. Check out the pricing list for more detailed information.

How do I get started?

We recommend that you contact us through our contact form and book a free of charge seminar where we help you get started. The seminar will be about one hour and in that time will show you how to use HIPER and the benefits you will have.
It is also possible to download HIPER and start using as a single user. At the moment, HIPER is going through beta testing. The official release of HIPER is 2016-01-01. If you want to participate in the testing phase, please contact is with our contact form.

What are the recommended system requirements?

You can run HIPER on any device.
Android application support Android handsets and tablets devices, OS version support 4 and above.
iOS application will support iOS handsets and tablets devices, OS version support 7 and above.

I’ve forgotten my password? How do I reset it?

Contact us through our contact form or mail us directly at hello@hiper.se and we will help you to reset your password.

I want to invite a colleague to HIPER, how do I do that?

Only the team administrator can invite members to a team. However anyone can sing up and start testing HIPER for personal use by clicking the “start here” button

How can I setup HIPER for use on my external monitor or TV screen?

From you mobile device you can use a chromecast, appleTV or similar device. From your computer you can do it by extending your screen to a hdmi, dvi or VGA supported screen or projector.
